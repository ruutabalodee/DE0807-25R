Our History

Husqvarna is one of the oldest motorcycle brands in the world, starting production in 1903.

The Early Years

Originally a weapons foundry in Sweden, the company shifted to bicycles and then motorized machines.

Racing Success

During the 1960s, Husqvarna became famous for dominating off-road racing and motocross championships.
